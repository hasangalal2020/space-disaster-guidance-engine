SDGE Architecture v1
Purpose

The Space Disaster Guidance Engine (SDGE) is a standards-aligned, space-enabled disaster guidance and decision support platform.

The objective is not only to detect disasters but to transform knowledge, standards, and space-based information into actionable recommendations.

Core Questions

SDGE aims to answer:

What happened?
What should be done now?
What should be prioritized?
Which space resources should be used?
Which international mechanisms should be activated?
What may happen next?
What if a specific action is taken?
