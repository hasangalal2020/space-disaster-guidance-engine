# Flood Guidance

## Overview

Floods are among the most frequent and destructive natural hazards worldwide.

## Preparedness

- Develop flood risk maps.
- Establish early warning systems.
- Identify evacuation routes.
- Train emergency responders.

## Response

- Monitor flood extent.
- Support evacuation operations.
- Assess affected populations.
- Coordinate emergency services.

## Recovery

- Damage assessment.
- Infrastructure restoration.
- Community recovery planning.

## Recommended Satellite Data

- Sentinel-1 SAR
- Sentinel-2
- Landsat
- MODIS

## Relevant International Mechanisms

- UN-SPIDER
- International Charter Space and Major Disasters
- Copernicus Emergency Management Service
