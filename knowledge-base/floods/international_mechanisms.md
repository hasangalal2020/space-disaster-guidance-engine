# International Mechanisms

## UN-SPIDER

Provides access to space-based information for disaster management and emergency response.

## International Charter Space and Major Disasters

Facilitates access to satellite imagery during major disasters.

## Copernicus Emergency Management Service (EMS)

Provides rapid mapping and geospatial information for emergency response.

## Sendai Framework

Supports disaster risk reduction through preparedness, response, recovery, and resilience building.
