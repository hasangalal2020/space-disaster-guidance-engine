Project Name

Space Disaster Guidance Engine (SDGE)

Owner

Hassan Abouseada

Vision

Develop an open-source, standards-aligned, space-enabled disaster guidance and decision support platform that transforms Earth observation data, international disaster management practices, and standards into actionable recommendations for preparedness, anticipatory action, response, and recovery.

Core Principle

SDGE is NOT a disaster detection system.

SDGE is a standards-aligned disaster guidance and decision support platform.

The objective is not only to detect hazards but to answer:

What happened?
What should be done now?
What should be prioritized?
Which space resources should be used?
Which international mechanisms should be activated?
What may happen next?
What if a specific action is taken?

Important Instruction for Future ChatGPT Sessions

When continuing this project:

Treat SDGE as a disaster guidance and decision support platform, not merely an AI or disaster detection project.
Prioritize:
Knowledge engineering
Guidance logic
Standards mapping
Governance frameworks
International mechanisms
Avoid jumping directly into machine learning models unless explicitly required.
Ensure all future architecture decisions support interoperability, explainability, auditability, and standards alignment.
Consider SDGE a potential reference implementation and demonstrator for future disaster management standards and best practices.
