SDGE Engineering Decisions Log
Decision 001

Title:
Knowledge-First Architecture

Status:
Approved

Date:
2026-06-08

Decision:

SDGE shall begin with a structured knowledge base and guidance framework before implementing AI or machine learning models.

Rationale:

The primary objective of SDGE is guidance and decision support rather than hazard detection.

Impact:

Faster development
Better explainability
Easier standards alignment
Easier validation

Decision 004

Title:
SDGE Is Not a Disaster Detection Platform

Status:
Approved

Date:
2026-06-08

Decision:

SDGE is a guidance and decision support platform.

Hazard detection systems may be integrated in the future but are not the primary purpose.

Impact:

Maintains focus on actionable recommendations rather than model development.

Decision 006

Title:
Multi-Hazard Scope

Decision:

SDGE shall support both natural and human-induced disasters.

Initial scope:

- Flood
- Earthquake
- Wildfire
- Drought
- Cyclone
- Landslide
- Volcanic Eruption
- Conflict-Induced Emergencies

Rationale:

Support comprehensive disaster risk management and humanitarian operations.
## Decision 007

Title:
Context-Aware Guidance

Status:
Proposed

Decision:

Future SDGE guidance should consider contextual factors including:

- Country
- Region
- Capacity
- Vulnerability
- Available Resources

Rationale:

Disaster guidance should be adapted to operational realities.
