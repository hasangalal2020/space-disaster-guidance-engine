Space Disaster Guidance Engine (SDGE)
Vision

To build an open-source space-based disaster guidance and decision support system that helps governments, disaster managers, humanitarian organizations, and researchers identify the most appropriate actions, satellite resources, and response mechanisms during all phases of disaster management.

Mission

The Space Disaster Guidance Engine (SDGE) aims to bridge the gap between space technologies and operational disaster management by transforming scientific knowledge into actionable guidance.

Objectives
Support disaster preparedness, response, recovery, and mitigation.
Recommend suitable Earth observation data sources.
Guide users toward internationally recognized practices.
Integrate knowledge from UN-SPIDER, Sendai Framework, IEEE standards, and global disaster management practices.
Build an open-source knowledge base for space-enabled disaster management.
